Benefits of Working at KLAY

At KLAY, We believe, Happy Employees = Happy Children = Happy
Customers! Therefore, we provide benefits beyond the basic necessities
like medical insurance & flexibility that support a balanced life.





Staff Child Care Medical Insurance/ESI Provident Fund Annual Bonus Attendance

In order to enable employees to bring their children to work and provide them with world class education, we have an employee-child education
policy in place. Employees with young children who fit into the age group and schooling programmes offered in the Centre they are working
at, can enrol their child in to their age specific programmes. The fee for the schooling/day care program will be subsidised.
